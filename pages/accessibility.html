Accessibility Statement

Updated as of May 2025

Fncher is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

1. Our Commitment

We aim to make our platform as accessible as possible. Our design and development practices incorporate accessibility principles aligned with the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines.

2. Accessibility Features

Fncher includes features such as keyboard navigation, screen reader support, color contrast considerations, and responsive layouts to improve usability for all users.

3. Ongoing Efforts

We regularly test and update our website to ensure continued compliance and accessibility improvements as technology and standards evolve.

4. Feedback & Contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our site or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at accessibility@fncher.com. We value your input and will respond promptly.
