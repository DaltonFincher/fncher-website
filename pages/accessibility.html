Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: May 12, 2025

1. Our Commitment

Fncher is committed to making our website and services accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

2. Features

  • Keyboard navigable interface for all interactive elements.
  • Alt text for all images and descriptive link titles.
  • Semantic HTML and ARIA roles for screen reader compatibility.
  • Consistent and clear layout for ease of use.

3. Ongoing Efforts

We continuously test our website for accessibility issues and make improvements as technology evolves. Our development process includes regular audits and user feedback integration.

4. Assistive Technology

Fncher is compatible with screen readers, voice recognition software, and other assistive technologies. We aim to support the most commonly used systems to ensure a smooth experience for all users.

5. Need Assistance?

If you have difficulty accessing any part of our site or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: contactfncher@gmail.com
  • We will do our best to accommodate your needs and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Future Improvements

We are committed to ongoing improvements to ensure full accessibility for all users. Feedback and input are always welcome as we strive to enhance our user experience.
