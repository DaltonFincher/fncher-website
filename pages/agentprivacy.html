Agent Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Fncher values the privacy of all professionals using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information from agents who register and offer services on Fncher.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, contact information, real estate license number, biography, profile image, and availability.
  • Task Activity: Task applications, communication logs, completion history, earnings, and client reviews.
  • Device and Usage Data: IP address, browser type, location (if permitted), and behavior while using our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify agent identity and licensing credentials.
  • To match agents with suitable homeowner requests.
  • To improve the platform’s services and reliability.
  • To process payments, communicate platform updates, and offer support.

4. Sharing Your Information

Your profile and public reviews may be visible to homeowners on the platform. We only share necessary information with third-party services (e.g., payment processors or background check providers) to support the platform's functionality.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Data Security

We maintain strict security protocols to protect agent information. While we work to secure your data, no system is immune to risk, so please use strong passwords and remain vigilant.

7. Profile Management

You can edit your agent profile, availability, and preferences at any time. You may also request to deactivate your account by contacting support.

8. Eligibility

Only licensed agents 18 years or older may use the platform to offer services. We reserve the right to verify credentials or remove profiles that violate our terms.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may change over time. We'll notify you of significant changes, and your continued use of the platform signifies your acceptance.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Agent Privacy Policy, please contact us at contactfncher@gmail.com.
